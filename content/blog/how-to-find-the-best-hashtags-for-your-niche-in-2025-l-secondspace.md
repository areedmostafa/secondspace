#### 3. Become a Hashtag Researcher: Where to Find Them



Great hashtags aren't found by chance; they're discovered through research.

#### 4. Hashtag Best Practices for 2025



* Quality Over Quantity: The 30-hashtag limit isn't a target. A set of 8-15 highly relevant, well-researched hashtags will always outperform 30 random ones.
* Customize for Every Post: Don't use the same block of hashtags on every single post. This can be flagged as spammy behavior. Tailor your list to the specific topic of your photo, video, or carousel.
* Caption vs. First Comment: The great debate is over. The algorithm doesn't care. Placing them in the first comment can create a cleaner-looking caption, but it has no impact on reach. Do what feels right for your brand's aesthetic.
* Avoid Banned or Broken Hashtags: Before using a hashtag, search for it. If the page shows a message saying posts are hidden, that tag is broken. Using it will kill your reach for that post.
